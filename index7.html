Cyberbullying stats




25% of people who have been cyberbullied are victims of it more than once.

The most common device used to cyberbully is the cell phone.

Girls are twice as likely when compared to boys to be cyberbullying victims.


Cyberbullying victims are two to nine times as likely to consider suicidical actions.

Only 10% of cyberbullying victims will tell an adult about the problem.

50% of all adolescents have been apart of or the victim of cyberbullying.


Spreading rumors is one of the most common type of cyber bullying even though many people believe that it is harmless.

Less than 20% of cyber bullying cases are brought to the attention of law enforcement.
